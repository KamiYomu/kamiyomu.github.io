# Your Collection

<img src="{{ '/assets/tutorial/see-collection-mangas.gif' | relative_url }}" height="300"/>

**Your Collection** showcases all the manga currently being downloaded that you've added from the [Download screen]({% link docs/Installation/download-mangas.md %}).

<img src="{{ '/assets/tutorial/download-mangas.gif' | relative_url }}" height="300"/>

## Adding Manga to Your Collection

When you add a manga to your collection, the download process begins immediately. You can monitor the download progress in real-time directly within your collection view.
